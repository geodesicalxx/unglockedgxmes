Embark on a thrilling heist adventure with Bob the Robber 4, a captivating browser-based game that puts you in the shoes of a cunning thief. With its engaging storyline and challenging gameplay, Bob the Robber 4 offers an immersive experience directly in your web browser.

Controls

Master the art of stealth and cunning with these simple controls:

  • Use the left arrow key to move left.
  • Use the right arrow key to move right.
  • Use the up arrow key to perform various actions.
