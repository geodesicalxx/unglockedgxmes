How to Play

In 1v1.lol, you must use your mouse to aim and WASD keys to move around the arena. Utilize Left-Shift to crouch and gain tactical advantages in combat. Switch between different weapons by pressing F, 1, 2, or 3, and cycle through building blocks using Z, X, V, or C keys. Additionally, rotate building blocks with the R key to create effective defensive structures or vantage points during battles.
