Get ready for an adorable and thrilling adventure with "Duck Life 4," a delightful browser game where you train and race your very own ducks. With charming graphics and addictive gameplay, Duck Life 4 offers fun for players of all ages.

How to Play

Basics

Train your duck's core skills in running, flying, climbing, and swimming to prepare for racing tournaments. Collect coins during training to purchase duck feed and unlock cosmetic items like hats and new ducks for your team.
