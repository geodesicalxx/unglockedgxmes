Embark on an epic journey through the annals of history with "Age of War," a thrilling strategy game that spans the ages from the dawn of civilization to the distant future. In this immersive game, players must evolve their armies, build defenses, and conquer their opponents across different eras. With its blend of strategy, action, and time-traveling adventure, "Age of War" promises an exhilarating gaming experience like no other.

Game Guide

Here's a guide to the controls in "Age of War":

Controls

Left click to deploy a unit.

With just a simple left-click, you can unleash your units onto the battlefield and lead them to victory. Mastering the art of unit deployment is key to outmaneuvering your enemies and claiming dominance throughout the ages. So rally your troops, forge alliances, and rewrite history in "Age of War!"
