Embark on a hilarious multiplayer adventure with "House of Hazards," a zany browser game that will keep you and your friends entertained for hours. Navigate through a series of absurd challenges and outlandish scenarios as you compete against each other to emerge victorious in this wacky house of chaos.

How to Play

Player 1 Controls:
Move left - A
Move right - D
Jump - W
Crouch/Fire - S

Player 2 Controls:
Move left - J
Move right - L
Jump - K
Crouch/Fire - I
